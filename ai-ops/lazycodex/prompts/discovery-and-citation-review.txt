You are reviewing the GYEOL repository.

GYEOL is a local fact registry for AI, search engines, and humans.
It is not an AI wiki.

Non-negotiable:
- Static-first rendering is mandatory.
- Core document content must be readable from raw HTML without client-side JS.
- schema-v3.sql is the source of truth.

- documents.data is rendering convenience only.
- No fake facts.
- Unknown facts must remain "확인 필요", low confidence, and not citable.
- Verified claims must have sources and last_verified_at.
- Public submissions do not require login.
- Never store raw IP addresses.
